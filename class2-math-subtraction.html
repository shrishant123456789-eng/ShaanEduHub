3.2 Subtracting Big Numbers (Up to 999)

We can subtract 1-digit, 2-digit, and 3-digit numbers.

Important Rule: Always start from the Ones place (Right side) and move to the left!

  • H = Hundreds
  • T = Tens
  • O = Ones

3.3 Subtraction Without Borrowing üòä

This is easy! The top number is bigger than the bottom number.

Example: 654 ‚Äì 321

Step 1 (Ones): 4 ‚Äì 1 = 3
Step 2 (Tens): 5 ‚Äì 2 = 3

3.4 Subtraction With Borrowing (Knock Knock!) üö™

Sometimes, the top number is smaller than the bottom number. You cannot take away 8 from 2.

So, we knock on the neighbor's door and BORROW 1 ten.

Example: 52 ‚Äì 38
(We cannot do 2 ‚Äì 8)

1. Borrow: The 2 asks the 5 (Tens) for help.
2. Change: The 5 becomes 4. The 2 becomes 12.
3. Subtract: 12 ‚Äì 8 = 4.
4. Tens: 4 ‚Äì 3 = 1.
Answer: 14

3.8 Number Line & Mental Subtraction üß†

Number Line: Start at the big number. Jump BACKWARD (left) by the small number.

Mental Maths Tricks:

  • Subtracting 0: Number stays same (5 ‚Äì 0 = 5).
  • Subtracting same number: Answer is 0 (9 ‚Äì 9 = 0).
  • Subtracting 1: Answer is the number before (Previous number).
