3.4 & 3.5 Multiplication Tables (2 to 10) üìä

Tables help us calculate without counting on fingers every time. You should learn tables from 2 to 10!

Fun Patterns:

  • Table of 2: Skip counting by 2 (2, 4, 6, 8...).
  • Table of 5: Numbers always end in 0 or 5 (5, 10, 15, 20...).
  • Table of 10: Just put a '0' after the number (10, 20, 30...).
